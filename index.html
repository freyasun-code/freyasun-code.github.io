1. Selfie: The Wrong Way vs. The Right Way

When you take a selfie very close to your face, the wide-angle lens exaggerates perspective. Features closer to the camera, like the nose, appear larger, while other parts like the ears or the sides of the face look smaller. This distortion makes the face look unnatural or less flattering. Taking a selfie from a slightly greater distance helps maintain more accurate proportions.
